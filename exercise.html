FIND YOURSELVES OUTDOORS
At GOFIT we believe in outdoor activities
and proper nutrition and research shows that people who play sports throughout their
lives are almost five times more likely to be physically active
than people who just hit the gym. That is why we
have curated a special activites and events for our customers.

CYCLING
Cycling combines physical exercise with being outdoors and exploring
new views. The benefits of cycling are almost as endless as the country
lanes you could soon be. A person burns about 700 calories per hour of cycling.
  • increased cardiovascular fitness.
  • increased muscle strength and flexibility.
  • improved joint mobility.
  • decreased stress levels.
  • improved posture and coordination.
  • strengthened bones.
  • decreased body fat levels.

HIKING
Hiking is going for long walks on an inclne.Walking
on an incline increases leg muscle activation,
stimulating the muscles of the calves,
hamstrings and glutes. A person burns about
450 calories per hour while hiking.
  • Lower your risk of heart disease.
  • Improve your blood pressure and blood sugar levels.
  • Boost bone density, since walking is a
    weight-bearing exercise.
  • Build strength in your glutes, quadriceps, hamstrings,
    and the muscles in your hips and lower legs.
  • Improve balance.

SWIMMING
Swimming is one of the most beneficial and fun sports.
In swimming, a person can burn upto 750 calories per hour.
  • keeps your heart rate up but takes some of the
    impact stress off your body.
  • builds endurance, muscle strength and cardiovascular fitness.
  • tones muscles and builds strength..
  • helps maintain a healthy weight, healthy heart and lungs.
  • provides an all-over body workout, as nearly all of your muscles
    are used during swimming.
